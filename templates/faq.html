General Questions

What is GoSkillNexa? +

GoSkillNexa is an AI-powered job platform designed to make job hunting smarter and more efficient. We connect talented professionals with top employers by analyzing skills from resumes and matching them to the perfect job openings.

Is GoSkillNexa free to use? +

Yes, for job seekers, all core features like resume upload, skill analysis, and job searching are completely free. We may offer premium services in the future.

For Job Seekers

How do I upload my resume? +

Simply navigate to the "Upload" page from the main menu. You can either drag and drop your resume file into the designated area or click to browse and select the file from your device.

What resume formats are supported? +

We currently support the most common resume formats, including PDF, DOC, and DOCX. For best results, we recommend using a PDF.

How is my data kept secure? +

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We use secure protocols and encryption to process and store your data. We will never share your personal information without your consent.
