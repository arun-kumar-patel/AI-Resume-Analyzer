Cancellation & Returns

Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

At GoSkillNexa, we strive to ensure our AI talent intelligence services meet your professional needs. However, if you are not satisfied, our refund and cancellation policy is outlined below.

1. Cancellation of Subscription

You can cancel your GoSkillNexa Pro subscription at any time. To cancel:

  • Log in to your account.
  • Go to Dashboard > Settings > Subscription.
  • Click on 'Cancel Subscription'.

After cancellation, you will continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time Pro subscribers if the platform fails to provide the promised AI matching results due to technical errors.

Refunds are not applicable in the following cases:

  • If the 7-day window from the date of purchase has passed.
  • If the user has already applied to more than 5 jobs during the Pro period.
  • Renewal payments are non-refundable.
Important: Refunds are processed back to the original payment method used on Razorpay.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please email us at billing@goskillnexa.com with your order ID and the reason for the refund. Our team will review your request within 48 hours.

4. Processing Timeline

Once a refund is approved, it may take 5 to 7 business days for the amount to reflect in your bank account or credit card statement, depending on your bank's processing time.

5. Failed Transactions

In case of a failed transaction where money is deducted but the Pro plan is not activated, the amount is usually refunded automatically by Razorpay within 24-48 hours. If not, please contact our support team immediately.

6. Contact Us

For any questions regarding refunds or cancellations, reach out to us:

Support Email: support@goskillnexa.com
