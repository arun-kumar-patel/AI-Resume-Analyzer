Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Welcome to GoSkillNexa. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our AI-powered talent matching services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and contact information.
  • Professional Data: Resumes (PDF/Doc), skill sets, and work history.
  • Payment Information: When you upgrade to a Pro plan, payment processing is handled securely by Razorpay. We do not store your credit card or bank details on our servers.
Note: Our AI engine analyzes your resume only to provide job matching scores and career insights. Your data is encrypted and secure.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your experience on GoSkillNexa:

  • To provide accurate AI-driven job recommendations.
  • To process payments and manage your Pro subscription.
  • To send important updates regarding your account or matching roles.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information with:

  • Verified Employers: Only if you apply for a job or choose to make your profile public.
  • Service Providers: Like Razorpay for secure billing.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your dashboard settings. If you wish to close your account, all your resume data will be permanently removed from our AI indexing within 30 days.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly.
